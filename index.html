About Us

Serenity in Mother Nature

Sundar Hotel & Resort is located in Majhitar, East Sikkim, and we strive to provide a luxurious stay where you can truly enjoy the serenity of our Mother Nature.

The Resort is targeted to provide a peaceful environmental experience and is equipped with the latest Infinity Swimming Pool fitted with a state-of-the-art filtration system. For health and wellness enthusiasts, the Resort provides a Steam Bath, Sauna, as well as a Jacuzzi to relax and rejuvenate the mind. It is not just for adults; Sundar Spa & Resort also features a Kids Pool and Multi-Play System for your little ones.

We are approximately 37 Kms from Gangtok and roughly 4 Kms from Rangpo. The closest Railway station is New Jalpaiguri (85 Kms away), and Bagdogra Airport is 90 Kms. The Resort is a vantage point for sightseeing travels to Gangtok (37 Kms), Namchi (44 Kms), Kalimpong (39 Kms), and Darjeeling (61 Kms).
